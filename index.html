Welcome to PN Cambodia

Passerelles Numériques Cambodia was the first project to be launched in 2005 in Phnom Penh.
PNC offers two IT training programs in System and Network Administration (SNA) and Web Programming (WEP) , based on a holistic educative approach including technical and soft skills, and a personal development program

PNC Values

Solidarity

It means standing together, helping one another, and working as one, especially in times of difficulty or for a shared goal.

Trust

It involves feeling safe to depend on someone, believing they will act in your best interest, and being willing to rely on them without constant doubt.

Respect

A feeling or attitude of admiration and consideration for someone or something, recognizing their worth, rights, or qualities, and treating them accordingly.

Responsibility

It involves accountability, reliability, and taking ownership of your behavior, choices, and their impact on others or yourself.

Canteen

The canteen offers a variety of affordable and nutritious meals, including both local and international dishes. Meals are prepared fresh daily, with options that cater to different dietary needs. The menu includes rice, vegetables, meat, and vegetarian choices, along with fruits and drinks. The canteen provides a clean and friendly environment for students and staff to enjoy their meals.

Salad – Fresh mix of greens, avocado, and cherry tomatoes. Light and healthy.

Grilled Meat – Juicy steak and sausages with roasted veggies. High in protein.

Noodles – Savory noodles with veggies and herbs. Flavorful and satisfying.

Spaghetti – Classic pasta with rich meat sauce. Hearty and delicious.

Our Sports

Playing sports improves physical health by keeping the body strong and fit, boosts mental health by reducing stress and building confidence, and enhances social skills through teamwork and communication. It also teaches discipline, time management, and resilience, which are useful in everyday life.

Football

Two teams of 11 try to score by kicking the ball into the goal. Only the goalkeeper can use hands.

Volleyball

Two teams of 6 hit the ball over a net to land it on the other side. Each team gets three touches.

Basketball

Two teams of 5 score by throwing the ball into a hoop. Players dribble and pass to score points.

Rugby

Rugby is played by two teams of 15. Players score by carrying or kicking the ball into the goal area. Passes go backward or sideways.

Aerobic Activities

Aerobic exercise strengthens the heart and lungs, improves circulation, helps control weight, boosts mood, and increases energy. Over time, it lowers the risk of chronic diseases and supports overall health.
